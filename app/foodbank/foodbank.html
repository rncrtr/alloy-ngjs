Love Abounds Food Bank


3rd Wednesday of the month
From 3:00 pm to 6:00 pm
at White Flag

Many families and school age children in and around our neighborhood don't get enough to eat, so this is a simple way to love our community in a tangible way.

We ask for donations of these items to give away once a month:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Grape and other flavors)
  • Pasta (Spaghetti and others)
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cereal

Please come out and join us to give away food. We pray and talk with them about their lives. We'd love to have you join us.
